HandyMeaning

HANDWRITING RECOGNITION AND INTERPRETATION (IMAGES AND MEANINGS)

September 2018

Idea

The idea is to create mobile application to recognize handwritten words and display the meaning and corresponding image of the word.

The project was done as a part of Creative and Innovative project in my UnderGraduate in 2 months time.

Vision

The vision of the project is to let users get the meaning of words effortlessly with better understanding, using relevant pictures. The users can focus the word which can be either handwritten or a printed text. When the word is recognized they get the meaning of the words and if they want to further look to the word they can get the image of the word.
