My name is Raaghavi Shanmugasundaram, but you can call me Raaghavi Shan. I hail from Erode, TamilNadu.

I am an engineer turned designer. I have always had a penchant for problem solving. With paper, pencils and steaming cup of coffee at hand, I feel nothing short of invincible! Finding solace among patterns and designs has made me reach where I am now.

I finished my B.Tech in Information Technology from College of Engineering Guindy, Anna University in 2019. Web design specifically piqued my interest during my college days and expedited my decision to pursue interaction design from IDC School of Design.

My key areas of interest are User Experience Design and Data Visualization. I find designing for children particularly enjoyable. I aspire to create more and more everyday, with relentless passion and joy.
